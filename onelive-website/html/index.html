SHOP DESIGN

Designing a shop is similar to how retailers display their collections as you walk through their stores. We believe collections provide the customers with the best overall experience finding and buying products.

MARKETING STRATEGY

Reaching your audience is critical to developing a successful shop. Marketing requires experience, tools and consistency. Mastering a strategy for search, social, and email is the key to year over year growth.

MOBILE STRATEGY

Mobile shopping is critical for today’s consumers. Conversions require developing a strategy to reach customers in their native applications. Including ad exchanges, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and Google.

MARKETPLACE STRATEGY

Over 55% of customers in the US start their product search on Amazon.com. 99% of entertainment brands are missing the boat on marketplaces because they don’t understand how to be successful through them.

PRODUCT STRATEGY

Products are what drive customer purchases. Creating fresh branded products monthly will increase your brands life time value with with each customer. Branded products are a statement to ones passion, so keep it fresh.

LOGISTICS STRATEGY

Giving your customers the fast shipping experience they love will help build customer loyalty and sales. Managing and fulfilling orders across channels can be extremely time-consuming, complex and can skyrocket your operational costs.

CHECKOUT

Checkout speed is critical and offering customers less clicks will dramatically increase conversions. From no typing ApplePay to quick pay platforms like PayPal, Amazon Pay, SMS pay are all fantastic customer experiences.

CUSTOMER SERVICE

Customer service is one of the most important aspects to running a successful shop. Most companies struggle with keeping up this part of equation because it requires staff, lots of staff to manage inquires from thousands of customers who just have questions.
